About Kawaii Pop is a Japanese band that defines itself as a “Cute J-pop” music. The band consists of Aiko (Vocal), Mai (drummer), Ayuko (pianist) and Komi (guitarist). The band is produced by Daiichi Kosho Company that produced their first album “Love Forever Grows”. In February 2019 their second album released famously known as “Sunset Dreaming”. Kawaii pop has also embarked on several tours, with a majority of their tour dates taking place outside of Asia. In 2020 Kawaii pop will be touring all over the US doing over ten concerts! Aiko, Mai, Ayuko and Komi hope to attract more american fans as they tour America.
